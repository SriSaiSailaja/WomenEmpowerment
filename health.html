Women Health

Women's health is a significant concern. Understanding menstrual hygiene and breast cancer awareness can save lives.

Menstrual Hygiene

Menstrual hygiene is crucial for health and well-being. It is important for women and girls to maintain hygiene during menstruation to avoid infections.Menstrual hygiene is crucial for the health and dignity of women and girls. It involves the practice of using clean menstrual products, maintaining hygiene, and managing menstruation in a way that allows women to lead comfortable lives during their periods. Lack of access to proper menstrual products, sanitation facilities, and education about menstruation can lead to health complications such as infections and reproductive issues. Proper menstrual hygiene also plays a significant role in breaking taboos and ending the stigma associated with menstruation. Ensuring that girls and women can manage their periods safely and hygienically empowers them to participate fully in education, work, and other activities without shame or restriction.
