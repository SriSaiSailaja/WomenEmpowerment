Adolescence in Girls

Adolescence is a crucial period in the life of every girl. It is marked by physical, emotional, and social changes that are an essential part of growing up. During this time, girls experience the onset of puberty, which brings with it hormonal changes and the development of secondary sexual characteristics.

Good Touch and Bad Touch

Good touch makes you feel safe, loved, and comfortable, like a hug from a family member or a high-five from a friend. Bad touch, however, makes you feel scared, uncomfortable, or confused, and it's important to speak up if someone makes you feel this way
