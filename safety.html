Women Safety

Women's safety is of paramount importance. Here are some legal protections in place for women:

  • The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013
  • The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005
  • The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961
  • Indian Penal Code (IPC) Section 354: Assault on women with intent to outrage her modesty

Self defence for girls

Self-defense empowers girls to protect themselves in dangerous situations by building confidence and awareness. It involves learning techniques to fend off attackers, but also emphasizes the importance of recognizing and avoiding potential threats. Developing self-defense skills not only enhances physical safety but also boosts mental strength and resilience.
